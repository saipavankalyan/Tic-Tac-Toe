Classic Mode Rules

If any of your 3 pieces form a line, you win!

This mode may end in a Tie if there is no winner when the board is full

You can place your piece in any of the open squares

First player has an advantage, as they get to put 5 pieces on the board

Endless Mode Rules

If all your 3 pieces form a line, you win!

This mode doesn't have a Tie. Play until a player wins

Each player have to first place their 3 pieces on the board before they can move their pieces

Players can move their respective pieces into any open square

A piece can traverse to it's direct left, right, top, bottom if and to the center if they are open

The center piece can move to any of the open square
